# MaRe Pitch Deck

## Slide 1: Title

MaRe  
One luxury scalp-health app with guest, internal, partner, and client experiences

## Slide 2: The Problem

- MaRe wins when the experience feels exclusive and precise.
- National growth usually pushes brands into generic outreach and low-quality content.
- That creates the personalized scale paradox: scale makes the brand feel less luxury.

## Slide 3: The Opportunity

- Wellness and scalp health are growing.
- Luxury salons need new ancillary revenue and stronger retail conversion.
- MaRe can become the system behind scalp diagnostics, education, and retail lift.

## Slide 4: Our Solution

- Guest mode for public exploration
- One sign-in for all MaRe roles
- Role picker for internal, salon-owner, and client dashboards
- Shared AI, storage, and review systems underneath

## Slide 5: Product One

Flutter app for live demos:

- one MaRe shell across iOS, Android, and Web
- role-aware dashboard routing
- visible AI watchtower and yellow-dot fallback marker

## Slide 6: Product Two

Simple Flask web app:

- Python-based and easy to adapt
- easy to share with judges or advisors
- same guest/sign-in/role model
- AWS S3 used as the image-storage target for pictures and generated assets

## Slide 7: Why It Wins

- protects brand fidelity
- increases prospecting speed
- increases outreach quality
- scales content volume
- turns AI failure into a safe review workflow

- more AI-search visibility
- faster content throughput
